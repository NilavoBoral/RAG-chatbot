--------------------
*** GOALS ***
--------------------

- Bot should answer general prompts (e.g., "hi", "good morning").

- Block LLM's knowledge (only answer from provided docs, OR from previous conversation).

- If questions asked from outside of provided docs, the bot will respond with: "No reliable source found."

- Add source only when the bot answers from the provided docs.


--------------------
*** Limitations ***
--------------------

- If token limit exceeded in a session, the bot will respond with: "API/token limit exceeded. Please start a new session."
